# Test: LaTeX examples

This post demonstrates basic LaTeX in Hugo Markdown.

Inline math example: Euler's identity $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$s inside a sentence.

Display math (centered):
$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2}\,dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

Quadratic formula:
$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

Aligned equations:
$$
\begin{aligned}
f(x) &= ax^2 + bx + c \\
f'(x) &= 2ax + b
\end{aligned}
$$

Note: Ensure your Hugo site loads KaTeX or MathJax for rendering (e.g., include the KaTeX JS/CSS in your layout).
